Using PMN as an AI Agent

A practical guide to deploying the framework through Claude, DeepSeek, and similar systems

Download PDF

The PMN framework can be loaded into any capable AI system as a persistent analytical lens. Once the model has internalized the document, it reasons through problems using PMN's evaluative criterion, ontological commitments, and diagnostic tools — rather than defaulting to its generic training priors.

The steps below move from setup to active use. The prompts are starting points; adapt them to your context.

01

Get the PDF

Download the latest version of the PMN Framework PDF from the link above. The PDF is the primary input — it contains the full text across all parts, which the AI will use as its working document.

Keep the file accessible; you will upload it at the start of each new conversation.

Note on versions. Always use the most recent version. Older versions are missing sections and contain superseded arguments. The version number appears on the first page.

03

Upload and prime the model

Start a new conversation. Upload the PDF. Then send the priming prompt below. Its purpose is to move the model from passive reading to active internalization — it should treat the document as a working toolkit, not a reference to summarize.

Priming prompt
I am attaching the PMN (Progressive Materialist Naturalism) Framework document. Read it in full and internalize it as your primary analytical lens for this conversation. Your task is not to summarize the document. Your task is to understand its architecture: — The epistemological foundation (Parts I–II): how PMN determines what counts as reliable knowledge and what exists. — The biological foundation (Part III): the minimal anchor — structural suffering — as the evaluative starting point. — The evaluative criterion (Parts IV–V): what counts as better or worse outcomes and why. — The structural analysis of power, institutions, and change (Parts VI–X). — The methodology (Part XII): how to apply the framework, including its stress-testing procedure and known failure modes. After reading, confirm your understanding by identifying: (1) the primary formula, (2) the three permanent conditions, and (3) the framework's core distinction from both liberal and Marxist traditions.
Why the confirmation request matters. Asking the model to identify specific elements forces it to demonstrate actual comprehension rather than pattern-matching on keywords. If the response is vague or incorrect, re-send the prompt before proceeding.
04

Assign the agent role

Once the model has confirmed comprehension, assign the operational role. You have two options depending on the complexity of what you want to do.

Single agent prompt
From this point forward, you are operating as a PMN analyst. This means: 1. You reason through all problems using the framework's evaluative criterion: does this arrangement reduce structural suffering and expand genuine becoming? If not, why not, and what would? 2. You apply the primary diagnostic: what does this system do when its predictions are contradicted? Does it revise or insulate? 3. You use multi-perspectival analysis before drawing conclusions — examine claims from the epistemological, material, power, and institutional layers. 4. You flag when a question falls outside the framework's scope or when you are extrapolating beyond what the framework can support. 5. You do not default to balance for its own sake. PMN has a direction. Apply it. Maintain this role for the duration of our conversation.
Multi-agent panel prompt
From this point forward, simulate a panel of three PMN analysts with distinct functional roles. When I present a problem, each analyst responds in sequence before a synthesis is offered. ANALYST A — Epistemological Auditor Examines the knowledge claims embedded in the problem. What is being assumed? What evidence would revise the analysis? Where is the framework being applied at the limit of its competence? ANALYST B — Structural Diagnostician Maps the material and institutional conditions: who holds leverage, what incentive structures are operating, what the power asymmetries are, and what the path dependencies constrain. ANALYST C — Strategic Assessor Applies the evaluative criterion concretely: given the structural analysis, what interventions are available, what sequencing makes sense, and what trade-offs cannot be avoided? After all three have responded, offer a synthesis that notes where the analysts converge and where genuine tension remains. Maintain these roles for the duration of our conversation. Label each response clearly.
05

Put the agent to work

The agent can now be directed at problems of any scale or domain. PMN is designed to be applied — not just discussed. Below are prompt patterns organized by use type.

Structural analysis
Analyze [situation / policy / institution / event] through the PMN framework. Specifically: — What are the material conditions producing this arrangement? — Who holds structural leverage and how is it being exercised? — What forms of suffering is this arrangement producing or sustaining, and are they preventable given current material conditions? — What does the primary diagnostic reveal about how this system responds to evidence that contradicts its legitimating claims?
Stress-testing a position
I hold the following position: [state your position]. Apply PMN's stress-testing methodology to it. Specifically: — What evidence would, if it existed, require me to revise this position? — What assumptions is this position making about material conditions, power distribution, or human biology that might not hold? — Is this position engaging in any of PMN's identified failure modes (section 12.5)? — What would a stronger version of this position look like after revision?
Generating a PMN position
What is the PMN position on [topic]? Do not hedge for the sake of balance. Apply the evaluative criterion directly and state what the framework implies. If the framework does not determine a clear position, say so and explain why — but distinguish genuine underdetermination from avoidance. Flag any place where you are extrapolating beyond what the document explicitly supports.
Open analytical inquiry
I want to think through [topic / question / problem]. Use the PMN framework as the primary lens. Begin from whichever part of the framework is most relevant — you do not need to apply every section. Go where the analysis takes you. I will follow up with clarifying questions.
06

Maintaining the role across a long conversation

AI models can drift from their assigned role over the course of a long conversation — particularly if they receive responses that implicitly reward generic rather than PMN-specific reasoning. Two recovery prompts.

Recalibration prompt
Your last response read as generic analysis rather than PMN analysis. Return to the framework. Specifically: apply the evaluative criterion explicitly, identify the structural conditions rather than surface-level causes, and — if relevant — apply the primary diagnostic to the claims you are examining.
Hard reset
Pause. Restate the PMN evaluative criterion, the primary formula, and the primary diagnostic in your own words. Then reapproach the last question from scratch using those tools.
Context window limits. In very long conversations, the model may lose access to early instructions as the context window fills. If this happens, start a new conversation and re-upload the PDF rather than continuing to recalibrate.
The framework is designed to be revised. If the agent produces an analysis that seems wrong, the more productive response is usually to stress-test the analysis using the tools in Step 5 — rather than to simply reject it. PMN's primary diagnostic applies to PMN itself.
